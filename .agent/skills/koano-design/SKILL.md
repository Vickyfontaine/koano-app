# KOANO design system

## Always check before building any UI
- Font: Neue Montreal only. Never Inter, Roboto, or system fonts.
- ALL section backgrounds: white (#FFFFFF) or pale-wash (#F0F7FC)
- ZERO dark section backgrounds anywhere on the site
- Brand blue #A8C4D4: section numbers, dots, chips, center lines
- Glass cards: hero section ONLY, placed over video render
- All buttons: pill shape, border-radius 100px, non-negotiable
- All primary CTAs end with ↗
- Visual reference: thefoundation.house — study before building
- Section numbers: 01 02 03 format, mono 11px, #A8C4D4, letter-spacing 1.5px
- Promise section: white background, navy text, no dark treatment
- Footer: white background, border-top only
- NO grey placeholder boxes ever
- NO stock photos, no Unsplash
- NO purple anywhere
- Glass cards from 21st.dev — do not generate custom glassmorphism
- 3D renders and video assets are pre-made — place them, never generate them
